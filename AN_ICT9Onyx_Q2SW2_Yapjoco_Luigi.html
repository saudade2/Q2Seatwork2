Top 10 Cat Breeds

Cat Breed Description
Siamese The Siamese Cat is from Thailand. It's known for its blue eyes, long and large ears, and a slender body.
Persian The Persian Cat is from Persia, but it was also imported to Italy in 1620. The Persian Cat is known for its thick coat, short legs, large eyes, and just being fluffy
British Shorthair As the name suggests, the cat came from Britain. The cat is known for its dense, but fluffy coat, and chubby face.
Bengal The Bengal cat is a domesticated cat. They are really attention grabbing, with them looking like small tiger, they are known for their gold spotted fur, and they like to play.
Maine Coon The Maine Coon is a domesticated cat. It's a really large cat, it's known for its build, dense coat, and looking like a "dog".
Birman The Birman Cat is from France, Burma. It has long hair, blue eyes, and fluffiness, It is basically like a Siamese cat but more fluffy and bigger.
Sphynx Contrary to it's name, one might think it's from Egypt, but it's origin is from Canada. It's known for being a furless and hairless cat.
Ragdoll The Ragdoll Cat came from California, United States. The cat has blue eyes, fluffy fur, and a large frame.
Abyssinian The Abyssinian Cat came from Southeast Asia. The cat is known for its long and lean build, and a personality to play, being described as "dog-like"
Russian Blue The Russian Blue came from Russia. It's known for its distinctive blue/grey fur color, it also has a dense and fluffy coat.
